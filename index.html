ABOUT US

Welcome to BOBA & BREW COMPANY!
Your go-to destination for refreshing and flavorful milk teas! Founded in 2025, we are passionate about delivering high-quality beverages made with the finest ingredients. Whether you're a bubble tea enthusiast or trying it for the first time, we've got something just for you!

At BOBA & BREW COMPANY, we believe that enjoying a delicious milk tea shouldn’t break the bank. Our drinks are not only masarap (delicious) but also sobrang affordable, so everyone can indulge in their favorite treats without any worries. Each drink is carefully crafted to ensure the perfect balance of flavors, sweetness, and creaminess in every sip.
